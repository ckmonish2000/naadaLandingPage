About Us

We are Naada

A global music and social media platform primarily
focusing on music.

Our vision for a truly radical new life requires every individual to re-think our thinking towards life and to make that happen we need to acknowledge each other and understand each other and if there is a language through which we all can understand each other is through Music.

Music has been, is there and will be.

Now, it is our duty and it is our responsibility as individuals with a vision of the future to bring each and every living being together to actively take part in creating a world of music.
